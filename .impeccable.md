# Design Context

- **Target audience**: Recruiters and engineers evaluating the author's data-engineering work via a public demo link.
- **Use cases**: Skim the project story in under a minute; explore the real graph; ask a live question and see the grounded, cited answer; judge the craft.
- **Brand personality/tone**: Editorial and precise. The page itself demonstrates craft: real data, provenance front and center, no marketing fluff. Light, paper-like, confident typography; mono reserved for actual queries and citations.
